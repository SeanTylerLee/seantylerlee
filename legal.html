Privacy Policy

Haul Path ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: Haul Path does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculations and data processing occur locally on your device.

Data Processing

  • Local Processing: All haul calculations, dimension checks, and weight validations are performed entirely on your device.
  • No Server Communication: The app does not connect to external servers or transmit any data.
  • No Analytics: We do not track usage patterns, user behavior, or collect analytics data.
  • No Third-Party Services: The app does not integrate with third-party services that could collect data.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: Any saved configurations are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not use iCloud or any cloud storage services.
  • User Control: You can delete all saved data through the app's settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

Haul Path does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for professional trucking and transport use.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through app updates.

App Description

Haul Path is a mobile application designed to assist trucking professionals in checking legal transport thresholds, weight limits, and permit requirements across different states in the United States.

Acceptable Use

  • Use the app for legitimate trucking and transport planning purposes.
  • Verify all information with official state authorities before transport.
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
  • Do not use the app for illegal activities or unauthorized access.
