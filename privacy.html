Privacy policy

Summary for MVP static demo — replace with counsel-reviewed copy before production.

What this tool does

The assistive permit preview is informational only. It does not replace official TxDMV / TxPROS systems or your permit documents.

Current MVP (browser-only)

In this deployment, PDFs are processed in your browser using embedded text extraction. Files are not intentionally transmitted to our servers for parsing unless we enable a backend later. Do not use this demo for permits you are not authorized to handle.

Future backend

If we add uploads, OCR, accounts, or route history, we will describe retention periods, deletion rights, subprocessors (e.g. hosting, OCR, maps APIs), and regional transfers in an updated policy.

Maps & third parties

The map is displayed with Mapbox (tiles and styles per Mapbox terms). Geocoding may use Photon; routing may use a public OSRM demo — each subject to its provider’s terms. Mapbox access tokens in the client should be URL-restricted in the Mapbox dashboard.
