Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AppHubPro for Mac ("AppHubPro", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the AppHubPro macOS application (the "App").

1. Overview

AppHubPro is a local productivity tool that helps you organize and store information about the software builds you are working on in one place. The App is designed to keep your data on your Mac by default and does not require an account or login.

2. Information We Store

AppHubPro stores information you choose to enter into the App, which may include, for example:

  • Project names, build names, and version numbers;
  • Notes about builds, configuration details, and checklists;
  • Paths or labels that you manually add to help you organize your work.

This information is stored locally on your device in the App’s data storage. AppHubPro does not automatically collect or send this data to any remote server controlled by us.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

AppHubPro does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking libraries. We do not collect analytics about how you use the App, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

4. Network and Cloud Services

The App does not automatically transmit your build information to any external service. If you choose to export, back up, or sync your data using systems provided by Apple (such as iCloud Drive) or other third-party services, that activity is governed by the privacy policies and terms of those providers.

5. Access to Files and Folders

If AppHubPro requests access to folders or files on your Mac (for example, to help you associate builds with project folders), that access is used solely to read or store the information needed for the feature you are using. The App does not scan your device for unrelated personal content and does not upload your files to our servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

AppHubPro is intended for professional and productivity use by adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

You can delete information you store in AppHubPro at any time from within the App (for example, by removing entries or projects). If you uninstall the App, locally stored data may be removed from your device subject to how macOS handles application data and any backups you maintain.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted means you accept those changes.
